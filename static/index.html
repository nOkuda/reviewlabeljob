Labeling Product Reviews

You will see 10 product reviews. Your task is to click on the number of stars you think the reviewer gave for each of these reviews. We are measuring both your speed and accuracy. We believe it will take, on average, about 30 seconds per review.

After clicking on the number of stars you think the reviewer gave, you will be shown the correct answer. In this case, click on the "Continue" button to continue with your task.

Do not press the back or forward button on your web browser.

Click the "Ready" button when you are ready to begin.
